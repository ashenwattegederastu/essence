October 24th, 2023

Dearest Julian,

I found myself thinking of our last conversation by the coast as the sun dipped below the horizon today. There was a particular stillness in the air that reminded me of the way the light caught the crests of the waves.

It has been far too long since we sat and shared these quiet moments. The world seems to move at a pace that demands we forget the simple beauty of a written thought. I hope this letter finds you well and in good spirits.

There is much more to say, but I shall leave it for the next time we meet. Until then, hold the memories close.

With warmth,

Eleanor
